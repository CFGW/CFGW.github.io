About this project

Shred4less

Shred4less is an online ski shop which specialises in high-quality mountain apparel at discount prices. They approached me looking for a logo and web design for their new ecommerce platform. At this stage they were also starting a blog to attach to the store. I offered and was comissioned to write a few articles for them as well.
