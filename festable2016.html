Festable

Festable is an annual music and arts festival held on Tasmania's North-West coast, which is hosted over three days, leading up to a massive New Years celebration. Festable inc. ordered an incredibly unique web design - no information, just an animated illustration of the mountains that graced the horrizon over their camping site.
