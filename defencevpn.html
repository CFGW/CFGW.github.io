About this project

DefenceVPN

DefenceVPN is a Virtual Private Network (VPN) company with a wide selection of servers to connect to. Their subscription service is jam packed with all the features you'd expect from on of the world's most reliable VPNs. I got in touch with them in their infancy offering my services as a digital designer. I stayed on as a marketing co-ordinator up until the company's sale in late 2017.
http://www.defencevpn.com
