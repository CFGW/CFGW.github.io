Gloomhaven X

Application development, UI/UX

WARNING: SPOILERS AHEAD

If you play Gloomhaven, and arenâ€™t familiar with the contents of Envelope X, turn back now. This project contains spoilers related to Envelope X and its contents.

Gloomhaven X is an application developed to facilitate players who have unlocked the contents of Envelope X, solved the cryptograph, and are permitted access to the Bladeswarm character.

In order to keep the character secret, the character model and associated player cards were not shipped with the game. While this solution certainly solves the problem of maintaining secrecy, it creates an obstacle in that it prevents players from playing the character.

A PDF of printable designs was made available for players to make their own cards for the character, but I desired a more elegant, digital solution.
