Shred4less

Digital Design, Content Production

Shred4less is an online ski shop which specialises in high-quality mountain apparel at a low price-point.

They approached me looking for a logo and web design for their new ecommerce platform. At this stage they were also starting a blog to attach to the store. I put my hand up, handled the design, and was commissioned to write a few articles to get them going.

The team at Shred4less love the outdoors. They came to me looking to upgrade their online game with clean, coherent webstore, and a logo with some momentum - something that indicates their intentions to expand from snowboards, to boards for all occasions.
