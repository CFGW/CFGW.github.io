Project01 Music

Digital Design, Brand Identity

Project01 Music is a community-based electronic music label and events collective based in Tasmania.

They got in touch looking for a designer to build an online hub for their events coordination and releases. What started as a clean, effective landing page quickly grew into the development of a new brand identity, email-marketing development, and some neat videography and motion-design for good measure.

Project01 already had a great artist collective within their network when they reached out. I had a great time collaborating with their team, developing and refining their vision, coordinating assets, and building a robust brand identity.
