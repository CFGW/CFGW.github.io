Fostering Fitness

Web Design, Brand Identity

Fostering Fitness is a personal training business located in QLD, Australia. They specialise in High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT).

Their team got in touch with me looking to develop their business with a new brand identity and a landing page to bolster their social media presence.

High Intensity Interval Training evokes a sense of rigorous activity; it's tempting to go for bold, fluorescent motifs. In QLD's over-crowded personal-training industry, this strategy has been employed time and time again - it no longer stands out.

I opted for a reserved, professional palette as a point of difference, and a welcome sign to Fostering Fitness's target demographic - the new-comer.
