About this project

Festable

Festable is an annual music and arts festival held on Tasmania's North-West coast, which is hosted over three days, leading up to a massive New Years celebration. I have worked with Festable inc. in the past. Some time ago they were forced into hiatus after a flood destroyed their site-grounds.

They touched base with me recently with plans to revive, and were looking for an identity to go with their revitilisation.
