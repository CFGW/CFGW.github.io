About this project

Fostering Fitness

Fostering Fitness is a personal training business located in QLD, Australia. They specialise in High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT). Fostering Fitness got in touch with me looking to develop their business with a new brand identity and a landing page to bolster their social media presence.
http://www.fosteringfitness.com.au

Palette

Typography

Identity

High Intensity Interval Training sure does evoke a sense of action, it's tempting to go for bold, fluorescent motiffs. In QLD's over-crowded personal-training industry, this strategy has been employed time and time again - it no longer stands out.

I opted for a reserved, professional palette as a point of difference, and a welcome sign to Fostering Fitness's target demographic - the new-comer.
